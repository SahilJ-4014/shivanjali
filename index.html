What We Offer

Our Services

🏊 Swimming Pool

Enjoy the feeling of rain even in summer with our refreshing swimming pool experience. Perfect for families and all age groups!

⛵ Boating Experience

Enjoy a serene boating experience in flowing water. Feel the breeze and connect with nature in a unique way.

🐂 Bullock Cart Ride

Experience traditional bullock and horse cart rides around the farm. Journey through scenic village paths and lush fields.

🏡 Comfortable Accommodation

Modern rooms built with traditional red bricks, tent stays, cozy cottages (huts), and unique treehouse rooms.

🎉 Adventures & Activities

Rain Dance, Farm Tours (Shivar Feri), Trekking, water waterfall experiences, and thrilling rope adventure activities!

🌿 Scenic Agro-Tourism Spread

Enjoy our 11-acre farm with suspension bridge experience and amazing photoshoots. Perfect for selfies and memorable moments!
